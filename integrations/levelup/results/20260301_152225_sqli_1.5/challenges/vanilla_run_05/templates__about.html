Our Mission

AuraFlow Media was founded in 1998 with the goal of digitizing the world's most vulnerable broadcast media. We maintain a climate-controlled vault containing over 2 million physical film reels and tapes.

Our engineering team works tirelessly to build the tools needed to index this massive collection for the next generation of storytellers.
