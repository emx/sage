About Veridian K&M

Founded in 2012, Veridian Kelp & Mariculture is committed to sustainable ocean farming. By utilizing advanced telemetry and automated harvesting vessels, we minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing nutrient density in our yields.

Our security-first approach to data ensures that all harvest metrics and vessel positions are protected by state-of-the-art filtering and encryption.
